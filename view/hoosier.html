Every section of campus has its own list of treasures.There’s a reason our campus is constantly winning accolades for its beauty. It is breathtaking. Soak up the sun at the Arboretum. Dip your toes in Showalter Fountain. Or tour the Collins Living-Learning Center and pretend you’re at Hogwarts.Everyone here has a favorite place. It won’t be long until you find your own.Hoosiers take athletics seriously. Whether you’re watching football, basketball, or any of the 22 other team sports at IU, you’ll notice that the stands are a sea of cream and crimson. And while every Hoosier fan prefers to win, in the end it doesn’t matter. It’s all about supporting your team—and being part of something bigger than yourself.As an IU student, you are part of a campus culture that values all backgrounds and perspectives. When we say everyone is welcome here, we mean it. Bloomington has a thriving arts scene, rich cultural opportunities, and endless opportunities to get out into nature.
